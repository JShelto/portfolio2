CraigsScout-SF

A merger of crime data and craigslist.com housing listings. Provides users with the percentage of crime that has occurred in each neighborhood over the past 3 months.

Wigwm

A hyper-local, map-based, event-planning web application. Allows users to create their own events and have other users RSVP to them. Created with a team of 4 other people in a single week.

CrunchMate

A recipe finder that allows users to search on the bases of time, ingredient, and course. Powered by the Yummly API.
